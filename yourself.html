How much do you know yourself? In life do we need to identify opportunities? No, we need to identify who we are and why we are and what we are going for and what motivates us to move forwar.

You will not be happy because you bought a car, you will not be happy because you bought a house, your life will not make sense because you traveled where you wanted or because you attended several parties and picked up several people and ate in expensive places.

Value those who love you, those who care about you, build good relationships and strengthen them, because, over time and in your old age, what will really matter is who is by your side, and those you did and makes a difference in their lives.
